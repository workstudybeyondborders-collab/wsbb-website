Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

Our fees vary depending on the service package and destination. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed breakdown of all fees involved, including our service charges and any third-party costs.

Timelines vary by destination and visa type. Study abroad applications typically take 3-6 months from start to arrival. Work placements can range from 2-8 months depending on employer processing times and visa requirements. We'll give you a realistic timeline during consultation.

Requirements vary by country and industry. Generally, you'll need relevant education credentials, professional certifications, work experience, and English language proficiency. Some countries require credential evaluation or additional licensing. We assess your qualifications and guide you on any gaps to fill.

No agency can legally guarantee visa approval as final decisions rest with immigration authorities. However, we maximize your chances through thorough document preparation, application review, and interview coaching. Our success rate is high because we only proceed with applications we believe have strong approval potential.

Yes! We identify scholarship opportunities that match your profile and assist with applications. While scholarships are competitive, we've helped many clients secure partial or full funding for their studies abroad.

We offer ongoing support including guidance on renewals, work permit extensions, career advancement opportunities, and any challenges you face settling in. Many of our clients stay in touch for years, and we're always here to help.
